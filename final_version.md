## Work Problem 
I work in the advertising industry in Audience Design & Data Science, and I want to solve the challenge of quickly and efficiently analyzing an "Index Report" that my colleagues and I export from a Data Management Platform (DMP) to pull insights on potential audiences. The report contains about 8 tabs, each with over 100K rows of attributes, so it can take hours to comb through. Over the last three years, I have been developing a script to extract the information we need, but it is only relevant to my account. I want to enhance the script so it works across account types such as Retail, CPG, Finance, Travel/Hospitality, and Pharma, and make it clean enough to be fed into an AI agent that will automatically clean and extract insights from the report. 
  1. The first step is to review the report and categorize the attributes by category, such as Demographics, Behavioral, and Shopping, and by account relevance, such as Retail or Pharma. 
  2. The next step is to update the script by removing its rule-based clause and replacing it with category and account relevance factors, then run a test report. 
  3. Finally, I want to feed the script to a new AI agent that can automatically clean and extract insights for someone who is not technical.

## How Generative AI Can Help
- Generative AI could help classify report attributes into reusable categories and account-relevance tags much faster than a manual review.
- Generative AI could summarize the most important audience insights from each tab in plain language for non-technical teammates.
- Generative AI could power an AI agent that automates report cleaning, insight extraction, and first-draft recommendations across different client accounts.
